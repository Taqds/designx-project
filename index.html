FAQ

Questions? We Have Answers.

Everything you need to know before working with us.

How long does a typical project take?
Most websites are completed in 3–6 weeks depending on scope. E-commerce or complex custom applications may take 8–12 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline upfront before starting.
How is your pricing structured?
We offer both fixed-price project packages and monthly retainer plans for ongoing SEO or support. Every project starts with a free consultation to understand scope before we provide a detailed quote. No surprises.
How long does SEO take to show results?
Most clients see meaningful traffic improvements within 60–90 days, with significant results at 4–6 months. SEO is a long-term investment — but the compounding ROI is unmatched by any other channel.
Do you offer website maintenance and support?
Yes. We offer monthly maintenance plans covering updates, security monitoring, backups, and performance checks. We're here for the long run — not just the launch.
Will I be able to update the website myself?
Absolutely. We build all sites with user-friendly content management in mind and provide thorough training. You'll have full ownership and access from day one.
What makes DesignX different from other agencies?
We combine technical excellence, creative design, and strategic SEO under one roof — with a results-first mindset. We don't outsource, don't overpromise, and treat every client's business as if it's our own.
