FAQ

Answers to Your Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our web development and SEO process in Fort Lauderdale.

General Questions
How long does a typical project take?
Most websites are completed in 3–6 weeks depending on scope. Custom web applications may take 8–12 weeks. We provide a detailed project roadmap during the Discovery phase.
Do I own the website after launch?
Absolutely. You have 100% ownership of the code, content, and branding. We believe in building long-term partnerships, not holding your assets hostage.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Every project we build is mobile-first. We rigorously test across iPhone, Android, tablets, and desktop browsers to ensure a flawless experience for every user.
SEO & Strategy
Do you guarantee #1 rankings on Google?
No reputable agency can guarantee #1 rankings because algorithms change. However, we guarantee that our technical SEO and content siloing strategy follows the highest industry standards for ranking growth.
How do you handle local SEO for my area?
We specialize in geo-targeted optimization. For Fort Lauderdale businesses, we implement local schema markup, build city-specific content silos, and optimize your Google Business Profile for the Local Pack.
How long until I see SEO results?
SEO is a long-term play. While technical fixes can show results in weeks, major authority building and ranking shifts usually take 3–6 months of consistent effort to stabilize and grow.
Technical & Custom Development
What is the advantage of a custom website over templates?
Custom sites are engineered for specific business goals. They load faster (better Core Web Vitals), have no unnecessary code bloat, and provide a unique user experience that templates simply can't match. For businesses in competitive markets like Fort Lauderdale, custom is a major competitive advantage.
Do you provide website maintenance after launch?
Yes. We offer maintenance retainers that cover security updates, daily backups, performance monitoring, and minor content updates to keep your site running perfectly.
How do you handle website security?
Security is baked into our code. We implement SSL, firewall protection, secure login protocols, and regular malware scans to ensure your data and reputation are protected.
Can you integrate CRMs like HubSpot or Salesforce?
Yes, we specialize in third-party integrations. We can connect your website directly to your CRM, ERP, or email marketing platform to automate your lead capture and sales funnel.
What tech stack do you use for web development?
We are experts in Modern Web Tech. Depending on the project, we use React, Node.js, Next.js, or custom PHP/WordPress. Our goal is always to choose the tool that delivers the best performance and scalability.
Local SEO & Growth
How do you optimize for Fort Lauderdale local search?
We use hyper-local keyword targeting, optimize your Google Business Profile, implement local schema markup, and build authoritative local citations. This ensures your business appears in the "Map Pack" for South Florida searches.
Is content writing included in SEO services?
Yes, high-quality, SEO-optimized content is a core pillar of our strategy. We write authoritative blog posts, service pages, and landing pages designed to rank and convert.
